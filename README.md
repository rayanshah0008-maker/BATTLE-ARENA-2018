**A Complete Free Fire Style Battle Royale Game for Android**

## 📱 Game Details

```
Platform: Android (Landscape Mode)
Players: 50 per match
Game Modes: Solo, Duo, Squad
Type: Battle Royale
Status: In Development
Free: Yes (Completely Free)
```

## ✨ Features

### Core Gameplay
- ✅ 50 Player Battle Royale
- ✅ Drop from Helicopter
- ✅ Large Open Map (Bermuda Style)
- ✅ Shrinking Safe Zone
- ✅ Real-time Multiplayer

### Weapons & Items
- ✅ 15+ Weapons (AR, Sniper, Shotgun, Pistol, SMG)
- ✅ Grenades & Equipment
- ✅ Health Items (First Aid, Energy Drink)
- ✅ Armor System (Vest, Helmet)
- ✅ Ammo Management

### UI/UX (Free Fire Style)
- ✅ Lobby System
- ✅ Match HUD
- ✅ Kill Feed
- ✅ Leaderboard
- ✅ Settings Menu
- ✅ Inventory System
- ✅ Mini Map

### Game Modes
- ✅ Solo Mode
- ✅ Duo Mode
- ✅ Squad Mode (4 Players)

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start Game** - Click Play on Main Menu
2. **Select Mode** - Solo, Duo, or Squad
3. **Wait for Players** - Lobby fills with 50 players
4. **Drop** - Choose landing location
5. **Loot** - Find weapons, armor, items
6. **Survive** - Avoid safe zone shrinking
7. **Fight** - Eliminate other players
8. **Victory** - Be the last one standing
